Optimizing Maternal Mental Healthcare: Educating Healthcare Professionals and Enhancing Utilization of the Healthy Beginnings Program for Pregnant and Postpartum Women

This DNP project aimed to create a consistent process to ensure all mothers and healthcare professionals are well-informed about postpartum depression and that patients receive consistent, thorough, and timely care. As mothers, we felt deeply passionate about the importance of caring for postpartum mothers, with special interest in Cheryl Beck's Theory on Postpartum Depression.
