Born in Billings, MT, Megan completed her Bachelor of Science in Nursing at University of Mary in Bismarck, ND. She currently resides in Helena, Montana with her husband, two daughters, and goldendoodle. She started her nursing career in Bismarck on a neurosurgical/general surgical floor in 2016. She met her husband, Erik, at a wedding in Montana, eventually moving to Oklahoma to continue her nursing career in a perioperative unit. After getting married in 2019, Megan and her husband moved to Wellington, New Zealand for an amazing opportunity. She obtained her nursing license there, practicing for 2 years on a general surgery, vascular, and ENT ward. Megan and her husband moved back to the U.S. six months pregnant to be closer to family and to start working on her Doctor of Nursing Practice Family Nurse Practitioner in May of 2021. She is continuing her education through the University of Mary with expected graduation April, 2025. Megan comes from a long line of nurses following both her mother and grandmother into the profession. At present, Megan is taking on motherhood and school. She ran collegiately and still continues to enjoy running with her goldendoodle, spending time with her family at their lake cabin in Montana, and traveling. Scotland is the next destination for a vacation, and first international trip as a family of four.
